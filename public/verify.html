Connect WhatsApp

Watobot sends messages as you, from your own WhatsApp number. To allow this, you need to add Watobot as a Linked Device — the same way WhatsApp Web works.

🔒

Your credentials stay private. After scanning, your WhatsApp session is encrypted with AES-256 using your personal auth token. Not even the server owner can access it. Your token is stored only in your browser — never on the server.

🌐

Your account is protected. Watobot connects to WhatsApp through a residential IP address near your location, so WhatsApp sees a local device rather than a shared server. Every user gets a dedicated IP — your account is never associated with others.

Watobot uses this to route your WhatsApp connection through a residential IP near your location, making the device look genuine to WhatsApp.
