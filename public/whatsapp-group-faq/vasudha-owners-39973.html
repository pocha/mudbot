Q. How can I get a water connection and what are the charges?

chat Share on WhatsApp

Water connection for each individual plot has already been laid by the builder and is available for use. No separate application is currently required to obtain a connection. As discussed in the last owners’ meeting: Construction Water Water used for construction is charged at ₹3,000 per month or Plot owners are requested to plan their construction water requirements in advance so that Vasudha builder team or the respective contractor’s team can arrange for a water tanker as needed.
