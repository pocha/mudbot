Q. What are the maintenance charges and what do they cover?

Maintenance is charged at 30 paise per sq ft per month (Option 1) or 20 paise per sq ft (Option 2). Option 1 includes common area maintenance and plot maintenance, while Option 2 covers only common areas.

Q. What is the procedure for waste management and plastic disposal at the farm?

There is no formal solid waste management system in the village. Owners are currently taking their non-compostable waste back to the city for disposal. Wet waste can be managed via a small composter on your plot.

Q. What is the process for getting a temporary electricity connection for construction?

Apply for a temporary connection through the TANGEDCO app or TNEB. A security deposit of approximately Rs 19,400 is required, plus fixed charges. Once construction is complete, you must request a conversion to a domestic connection.
