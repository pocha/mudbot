Q. What is the maintenance fee structure and what does it cover?

Maintenance is charged at 30 paise/sq ft for full service (Option 1) or 20 paise/sq ft for common area maintenance only (Option 2). Payments are currently requested via Manju's UPI until a dedicated bank account is created.
